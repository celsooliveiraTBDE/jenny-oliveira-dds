Meet The Team

Meet Dr. Oliveira

Dr. Jennifer Oliveira (formerly Dr. Akagi) has a vivid passion for her work as a dentist. Her warm nature along with her easygoing personality allows her patients to feel confident and comfortable. Caring and meticulous about the details, she strives to offer her patients the utmost in treatment and technology available today. She is a member of the American Dental Association as well as the California Dental Association and the Los Angeles Dental Society. She regularly takes part in the latest continuing education programs and tries to give back to the community. She also mentors students looking to become future dentists. Dr. Oliveira has been treating patients for over ten years and looks forward to many more years of providing personal, caring service to her family of patients.

Meet Dr. Dogra

Dr. Meenu Dogra received her Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from the Schulich School of Medicine and Dentistry at the University of Western Ontario. She returned to her native home of Los Angeles after practicing general and hospital dentistry in Ontario for over five years. She truly loves educating patients about their oral health to help maintain healthy smiles. She strives to create a calm and caring environment to ensure that her patients are well taken care of and at ease. She uses advanced materials and techniques and is continually updating her skills and knowledge to provide patients with the best care possible. When not in the office, Dr. Dogra enjoys traveling, attending concerts, hiking, and spending time with her family, friends, and energetic doggy Tyson!

“I am so pleased to have Dr. Dogra as a part of our practice family! Our team is a group of people who genuinely care for each other and for our patients and this reflects in our patient referrals and our overall office success. Therefore, is it no surprise that Dr. Dogra is a perfect fit as a leader and healthcare provider within our dental family. Dr. Dogra is my own personal dentist and has the same overall gentle touch and technique when it comes to dentistry. She is extremely thorough and friendly, which is a combination that is a necessity in the dental field. She always strives for her patients to fully understand their treatment needs. Also, Dr. Dogra is so calm and patient which is comforting to anyone with a dental phobia. She is extremely considerate of all individual needs and requests. It is no wonder she is so well liked. Overall, she is soft spoken and respectful and an absolute delight to be around.”

"

Dr. Oliveira Santa Monica Dentist
